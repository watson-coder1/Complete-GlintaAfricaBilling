Payment Information

Welcome to Glinta Africa! Here's how to make payments for our services:

M-Pesa Payment Instructions:

  1. Select your desired internet package
  2. Enter your M-Pesa registered phone number
  3. You will receive an STK push notification on your phone
  4. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the payment
  5. Once payment is confirmed, your internet access will be activated automatically

Payment Methods Accepted:

  • M-Pesa: Instant activation via STK Push
  • Paybill: Use our business number and your username as account
